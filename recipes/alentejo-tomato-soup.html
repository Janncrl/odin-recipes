Alentejo Tomato Soup

Description

Soups of Alentejo origin have an unparalleled gastronomic richness, tomato soup is no exception!

Tomato soup is one of the most beautiful examples of the beautiful Alentejo region . The base is, of course, yesterday's bread cut into thin slices (according to taste) to which is added a rich broth made with quality ripe tomatoes. Eggs are added, adding a source of protein to this beautiful and rich tomato broth. It is flavored with garlic and onion and olive oil is the base of its seasoning. Delicious and filling, this soup, made with local ingredients, will comfort your stomach and soul on the coldest days of the Alentejo winter!

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 4 Ripe Tomatoes
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 2 Eggs
  • QB Slices of Alentejo Bread
  • QB Olive Oli
  • QB Salt

Steps

  1. Start by chopping an onion and browning it. Meanwhile, cut 4 ripe tomatoes, add 1 bay leaf and 1 clove of garlic to the stew, and then the tomato.
  2. Season with salt and let it cook a little more.
  3. Meanwhile, cut 2 potatoes into pieces, add to the pan, and cover with water.
  4. Cover and let cook for 15 minutes.
  5. After this time, pour 2 eggs into the soup, and cover again, so that they are well poached.
  6. Finally, cut slices of Alentejo bread and make a bed to receive this tasty tomato soup.
